About Neo

A group of businesses, held to one standard.

Neo Homes began as a way to bring together ventures that share a single instinct — treat the people, places, and spaces we build with quiet, unmistakable care.

Today that instinct runs through everything we do. We don't believe in shortcuts. We believe in compounding good decisions, in listening longer than we speak, and in building structures that age beautifully — like well-cured concrete, seasoned timber, or a view that improves with every year.
